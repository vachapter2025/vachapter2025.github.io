Parking Information:

Parking on Campus

Parking on Virginia Tech campus uses the ParkMobile app. Detailed instructions for downloading and using ParkMobile can be found at https://parking.vt.edu/parking/parkmobile.html

  • Zone code for Parking lots: 85101
  • Zone code for Parking garages: 85117
  • Parking options: 1 hr, 2 hr, or whole day
  • Whole-day parking cost: $8 (or $10 for some garages) + < $1 transaction fee

With proper payment on ParkMobile, visitors to campus can park at any parking spot on campus except for the Service or 24-hr F/S ones.

Closest Parking to Graduate Life Center:

Squire's Parking Lot and Architecture Annex Lot - These lots can fill up depending on the time

Perry Street Parking Garage ($10/day) - Always has spaces, but it's a 5-10 minute walk from the GLC


Town of Blacksburg Parking

The GLC is close to Blacksburg downtown, which offers the options of metered parking and garage parking.

  • Metered Parking Rate: $1/hr with a limit of 2 hours maximum
  • Kent Square Garage: Within 10-minute walk from the GLC
